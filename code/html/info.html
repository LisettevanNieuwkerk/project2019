On the wine reviews page a dashboard is displayed visualizing the data of 110.000+ wine reviews.
A worldmap displays the average wine rating per country, also the amount of reviewed wines per country is shown.
In the scatterplot the price quality ratio of the wines is displayed and in the circularpacking the different varieties of the wines
are visualized. A selection can be made for the years when the wines are produced. With the slider a minimum year and a maximum year
between 1990 and 2016 can be selected. A specific country can be selected on the worldmap or with the drowdown menu. Also
a certain variety van be selected in the circularpacking. By clicking on the dots on the scatterplot the corresponding
wines will be shown.

This dashboard is meant for people who are interested in wine and can be used to find the best quality price wine of a certain year, country and/or variety.
